Q1. Why sell my metals to Sristar Gold Company?
We offer competitive prices, transparent evaluation, and instant cash payments with no hidden charges.

Q2. How long does the whole process take?
The entire process typically takes 15-30 minutes from evaluation to payment.

Q3. How do you check the metals?
We use advanced German technology including spectrum analysis for accurate purity testing.

Q4. What is the charge for testing and purity evaluation?
Our testing and evaluation services are completely free of charge.

Q1. How many days before the event should I book jewellery?
We recommend booking at least 7-10 days in advance to ensure availability.

Q2. How long to receive jewellery after booking?
Jewellery can be collected within 24-48 hours after booking confirmation.

Q3. What is the rental duration (min/max)?
Minimum rental period is 3 days, maximum is 30 days.

Q4. What are your rental charges?
Rental charges vary based on the jewellery value and duration. Contact us for specific rates.

Yes, we help release gold pledged at banks, finance companies, and pawn shops.

Bring your pledge documents, we evaluate and pay the outstanding amount plus our service fee.
